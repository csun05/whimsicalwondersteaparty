Our Selection

Tea

Green Tea

Green tea has a rich history that is deeply rooted in Chinese culture. Green tea is made from Camellia sinensis leaves; the color can range from light yellow to green. The tea has a light, grassy, vegetative taste making it a great pairing with fruits or fruit tarts.

English Breakfast Tea

In the late 1800s, English Breakfast tea rose to fame in the UK. English Breakfast Tea consists of a blend of mulitple black teas. The depth and richness of the tea pairs well with lemon flavored deserts. Thanks to its robust, rich and full-bodied flavor, it is able to handle and compliment strong flavors.

Oolong Tea

Oolong is believed to have originated in China during the Qing Dinasty. It is made from Camellia sinensis leaves, but differs from green tea in the oxidation period. Oolong has a subtle floral, grassy taste. The tea has a complex flavors making it a great pairing with all tea snacks.

Earl Grey Tea

Originating from mid 1800s, Earl Grey was named after a Charles Grey. Many theories surround the tea's creation, but it is believed that the addition of bergamot oil was intended to enhance the taste of and aroma. Earl Grey has a floral tone with a notable taste of citrus. The sweetness from the begamot makes it a perfect pairing with tea sweets.

Sweets

Macarons

Dating back to the 16th century, macarons are delicate French pastries made from almond flour, confectioners' sugar, and egg whites. The cookies have an extraordinary texture. They are chewy and crunchy, but melts in your mouth. With a variety of flavored fillings, the possibilies are endless.

Strawberry Tarts

These strawberry tarts are cold and creamy, perfect for those hot summer days. These tarts are packed with strawberry puree, blended with cream cheese and whipped cream on top of a buttery shortbread crust.

Coconut Cakes

The scrumptious coconut cake is a delight for the taste buds, with its rich and moist texture complemented by the creamy and decadent buttercream rosettes. These coconut cakes are light and fluffy. The combination of the coconut flavor and the beautiful roses make this cake a visually appealing and irresistible treat.

Mini Cheesecakes

The mini cheesecake is a delightful treat that combines the sweetness of the cream cheese with a tangy twist. The creamy cream cheese filling sits on top of a crispy graham cracker crust. Its creamy texture adds to the overall indulgent experience, creating a perfect balance of flavors.

Tea Sandwiches

Cucumber Sandwich

Cucumber sandwiches are a simple and elegant way to elevate any tea party. These sandwiches feature thinly sliced cucumbers accompanied by cream cheese(or mayonanaise). The crisp refreshing taste of cucumber sandwiches are perfect for your afternoon tea.

Turkey Tarragon Tea Sandwich

The turkey tarragon sandwich is a hearty tea sandwich that is perfect for tea time. This sandwich combines the appetizing flavors of finely chopped turkey, almonds, and tarragon(a subtle hint of licorice-like flavor). This combination of flavors creates a pleasurable and unique sandwich option, perfect for those seeking a delicious and refreshing alternative.

Mozzarella, Tomato, Basil Sandwich

Don't let the simplicity of this sandwich trick you! This delectable sandwich is made with fine mozzarella cheese, thick slices of cocktail tomatoes, and fresh sweet basil. It is a simple sandwich with intricate flavors that your guest will love.

Egg Sandwich

The egg sandwich is a popular tea time sandwich choice. It if filled with creamy, soft, fluffy eggs mixed with mayonnaise, herbs and spices. It is a versatile dish that goes well with any tea of your choice. These mini sandwiches are sure to elevate any tea party.
